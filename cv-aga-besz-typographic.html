about

I've spent more than a decade building games and the teams behind them — from live mobile titles to a large, open-source 3D social platform. Along the way I stopped thinking of the job as just engineering and started running it like a business: I own the strategy and the budget, negotiate the engine and vendor contracts, and care as much about getting the product into people's hands as about the code underneath. I led a full ground-up rebuild of our client and grew the team from a handful of engineers into a real organisation with its own managers and QA. But the part I care about most isn't the tech — it's the people building it: I give them clarity, clear the obstacles, hire people better than me, and trust them. I lead with data over opinion, keep things simple, use AI as a real part of how we work, and I still read the code.
