Engineering Management Dashboard

A practical dashboard for engineering management: releases, CI health, Sentry signals, Unity Cloud costs, priority reviews, team load and AI-assisted summaries.

Problem

A team shipping frequently needs a reliable way to see what changed, what is risky and where attention is needed. This is a practical engineering-management dashboard, not a broad platform: scheduled agents summarize repository activity, releases, CI health, Sentry signals, Unity Cloud cost data, priority reviews and team load.

Goals

  • Turn repository activity into a daily management view.
  • Surface release, CI, Sentry and team-load signals.
  • Track Unity Cloud cost data alongside engineering activity.
  • Use AI-assisted summaries to reduce manual status gathering.
  • Support priority reviews without inventing a heavy process.

Interesting problems

  • How do you keep metrics from becoming vanity dashboards? Put them where priority reviews and engineering-management decisions happen.
  • How do you make AI useful for management work? Use it to summarize evidence and flag changes, while humans keep ownership of decisions.
  • How do you reduce operational load? Automate repeated status-gathering paths, but keep ownership explicit.

Lessons learned

  • A useful dashboard changes behavior: what gets reviewed, fixed, delayed or escalated.
  • AI summaries are most useful when tied to concrete engineering evidence.
  • Operational simplicity is a feature for the team.
