about

I'm an engineering leader with more than a decade of experience building consumer software used by millions of people, most of it real-time and mobile. At Decentraland I run engineering for a large open-source social platform: I own the technology strategy and budget, negotiate vendor contracts, and led a ground-up rebuild of our main consumer application, now the primary client in production. I grew the team from a handful of engineers into an organisation with its own managers and QA, and built the hiring process and career ladder behind it. I lead with data rather than opinion, care a lot about reliability and how fast we ship, use AI as a normal part of how we work, and I still read the code.
